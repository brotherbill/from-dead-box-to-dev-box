# Parental Responsibilities

Parents are responsible for ensuring that the young engineer uses the workstation safely, appropriately, and within healthy boundaries.

Parents must:

1. Enforce a daily **lights‑out time**.
1. Ensure the workstation is **fully shut down** at lights‑out, not placed in sleep mode.
1. Confirm that the young engineer does not use the workstation late at night or during inappropriate hours.
1. Maintain physical security of the workstation and its peripherals.
1. Use parental controls as needed to support these responsibilities.

These responsibilities are comparable to other non‑delegable parental duties, such as securing hazardous items and ensuring that children do not remain awake into the early morning on school nights.

This policy applies to all systems in this program.
