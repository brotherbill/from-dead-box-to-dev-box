# First Login and System Update  
###### C:/dev/repos/from-dead-box-to-dev-box/00-overview/05-first-login-and-system-update.md

## 1. Sign in to the parent administrator account

1. At the Sign In screen, select the parent administrator account created during installation.
1. Enter the administrator password.
1. The desktop will load and display the PopOS! environment.

---

## 2. Accessibility Setup (If Screen Reader Appears)

1. Some computers may automatically enable the Screen Reader during the first‑boot setup.
1. If the Screen Reader begins speaking or the interface appears in high‑contrast mode, this is not an error.
1. To turn off the Screen Reader:
   - Select the Screen Reader toggle.
   - Ensure the toggle is set to Off.
1. After disabling the Screen Reader, continue with the normal setup flow.
1. If the Screen Reader does not appear, proceed to the next section without changes.

---

## 3. Connect to the internet

1. Check the network icon in the top‑right corner to determine which connection method is available.
1. If Wi‑Fi is available:
   - Select the home Wi‑Fi network.
   - Enter the Wi‑Fi password.
   - Wait for the connection indicator to confirm internet access.
1. If Wi‑Fi is not available:
   - Plug an Ethernet cable from the home router into the computer’s Ethernet port.
   - The connection will activate automatically.
   - Confirm the wired‑connection symbol appears in the network icon.
1. After the computer is connected to the internet, click Next to continue.

---

## 4. Complete the PopOS! First‑Boot Setup Wizard

1. After signing in, PopOS! may show a short setup wizard.
1. This wizard is normal and is not the installer used earlier.
1. The screens may include:
   - Language confirmation  
   - Keyboard confirmation  
   - Privacy or telemetry options  
   - Online accounts (you may skip these)  
1. Accept the defaults unless you have a specific preference.
1. Continue clicking Next until the wizard completes and the desktop appears.

---

## 5. Run system updates

1. Open the PopOS! Shop from the dock.
1. Select the Installed tab.
1. Click Update All.
1. Wait for all updates to complete.
1. If prompted, restart the computer.

---

## 6. Prepare for creating the child account

1. After updates are complete, remain signed in as the parent administrator.
1. The next document will guide you through creating the child account and configuring the development environment.
