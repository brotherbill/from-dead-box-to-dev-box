# Create Standard Accounts

This step creates the two standard (non‑administrator) accounts that will be used on this computer:

1. The parent’s daily‑use account  
1. The student’s learning account  

These accounts do not have administrator privileges. This separation ensures safety, stability, and predictable operation.

**Note:** All Full Name and Username values shown in this document are examples. Replace them with the actual names you want to use for your household. For example, you may enter **Benny Smith** as the Full Name and **benny** as the Username. Usernames must be lowercase and contain no spaces.

---

## Create the Parent Daily‑Use Account

1. Click the **System Menu** in the top‑right corner of the screen.
1. Click **Settings**.
1. In the left panel, click **Users**.
1. Click **Unlock** and enter your administrator password.
1. Click **Add User**.
1. Enter the following example values (replace with your actual names):
   - **Full Name:** Best Parent  
   - **Username:** best-parent  
1. Set a password for the account.
1. Ensure **Administrator** is **not** selected. This must remain a standard account.
1. Click **Add** to create the account.

---

## Create the Student Account

1. While still in **Settings → Users**, click **Add User** again.
1. Enter the following example values (replace with your actual names):
   - **Full Name:** Power Student  
   - **Username:** power-student  
1. Set a password for the student account.
1. Ensure **Administrator** is **not** selected. This must remain a standard account.
1. Click **Add** to create the account.

---

## Sign Out and Verify Both Accounts

1. Click the **System Menu** in the top‑right corner.
1. Click **Power**.
1. Click **Log Out**.
1. On the login screen, confirm that you now see:
   - **Best Parent (best-parent)**  
   - **Power Student (power-student)**  
1. Log in to each account once to confirm that both accounts open successfully.
